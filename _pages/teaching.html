Courses Conducted

I have been serving as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Northern University Bangladesh since September 2022. I conduct both theory and lab courses, focusing on the practical integration of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Software Engineering. The courses I have conducted include:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Expert System
  • Database Management Systems (Theory & Lab)
  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Architecture
  • Software Development

My teaching emphasizes hands-on implementation, real-world datasets, and project-based learning to help students bridge the gap between theoretical understanding and applied problem-solving.

Research Supervision

Alongside teaching, I actively supervise undergraduate thesis and research projects that apply deep learning, computer vision, and AI for healthcare. Current and recent supervised projects include:

  • Enhanced Pediatric Dental Segmentation Using a Custom SegUNet with VGG19 Backbone — Accepted at ICCIT 2024
  • Multimodal Deep Learning for Handwritten Prescription Recognition with Explainable AI — Submitted to ICCIT 2025
  • Pediatric Teeth Segmentation from Dental Radiographs Using Transfer Learning
  • Autism Care Network – An online-based academic platform for autistic children
  • NUB Online Examination System – A web-based assessment management platform

Independent Research and Development

Beyond institutional supervision, I conduct independent research and system development projects focused on real-time AI applications, federated learning, and human behavior understanding. Selected works include:

  • Subject-Independent Emotion Recognition from EEG Signals
  • Cross-Domain Federated Learning for Skin Disease Classification
  • Real-Time Emotion and Attendance System – A Raspberry Pi-based deep learning system for classroom analytics
  • Conveyor Belt Fault Detection System – An industrial AI solution for British American Tobacco (BAT)

Through both my teaching and research mentorship, I strive to inspire students to explore innovative AI-driven solutions while continuously advancing my own research in human-centered and healthcare-focused artificial intelligence.
